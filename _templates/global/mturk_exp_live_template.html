Participate in a Market Experiment

Participants are needed for a Virginia Tech Economics Laboratory research study IRB: 22-124.

This research studies how market institutions affect asset prices. The experiment will be opened starting precisely at 1:55 PM EDT and will be available to the first 20 participants to connect to it.

About the experiment

Participants will place buy and sell orders for a stock in a simulated market with 19 other participants (20 in all). The experiment will take about 2 hours to complete and will consist of the market simulation and a brief survey.

Compensation for the experiment will be $12.00 for your time with a possibility to earn bonus payments depending on your performance in the market. To participate you must be 18 years of age or older and able to read and comprehend the English language.

Compatibility

  • The experiment must be run on a laptop or desktop computer.
  • We recommend using either the Chrome, Firefox, or Safari web browser. We cannot guarantee proper performance with IE or Edge.

If you have any questions, please write to the organizers of this experiment through the Mturk messaging system.
