Participants are needed for a Virginia Tech Economics Laboratory research study IRB: 22-124.

This is a pre-screening survey to determine participant availability for a forthcoming market experiment conducted by the VT Econ Lab at Virginia Tech University. This research studies how market institutions affect asset prices.

To ensure enough participation, we are running this survey to determine availability of mturk members. You will be shown a list of potential timeslots and will be asked to indicate which ones you will be able to attend. If we can run an experimental session during a timeslot for which you are available, we will invite you to participate in that session.

This survey should take no more than 5 minutes to complete, for which you will be paid $0.25.

If you have any questions, please write to the organizers of this experiment through the mturk messaging system. Thank you for your time and consideration.

About the main experiment.

Participants will place buy and sell orders for a stock in a simulated market with 19 other participants (20 in all). The experiment will take about 2 hours to complete and will consist of the market simulation and a brief survey.

Compensation for the experiment will be $12.00 for your time with a possibility to earn bonus payments depending on your performance in the market.

To participate you must be 18 years of age or older and able to read and comprehend the English language.

If you have any questions, please write to the organizers of this experiment through the Mturk messaging system. Thank you for your time and consideration.
