At the start of the experiment, you will be allocated an amount of CASH, in a fictitious currency we will call Experimental Currency Units, or ECU. At the end of each round, you will earn interest on the CASH you hold at a rate of 5%.

At the end of a round you hold 100 ECU in CASH. You will earn 5 ECU in interest.

STOCK is the risky asset. For each share that you own at the end of each round you will earn a dividend of either 0.4 ECU or 1.00 ECU. Either dividend is equally likely.

At the end of a round you hold 10 shares of STOCK and the dividend is 1.00 ECU per share. You will earn a dividend payment of 10 ECU.
