During each round, you will have the opportunity to place orders to BUY or SELL shares of STOCK.    An order consists of the order type (BUY or SELL), the number of shares desired and the price at which you are willing to BUY or SELL.

BUY 4 shares at a price of 1800 points

Or more compactly:

BUY 4 @ 1800

A BUY order sets a maximum price you are willing to pay.    That is, entering the above order indicates that you are willing to BUY 4 shares of the STOCK at a price of 1800 or below, but you will not be willing to BUY at a price greater than 1800.

A SELL order indicates a minimum price.    So, entering “SELL 3 shares at a price of 2000” means that you are willing to SELL at any price 2000 or greater.
