In this experiment, you will be allowed to borrow CASH to purchase STOCK. If you submit a BUY order that would cost more CASH than you currently have, the system will lend you the funds to cover the cost of the purchase.

You have 50.00 in CASH and enter the order (BUY 6 @ 10.00). This order will potentially cost you 60.00. Leaving you with a CASH balance of -10.00.

If your CASH balance becomes negative, you will be charged 5% interest. To continue the above example, at the end of the market period, you will be charged 10.00 * 5% = 0.20 in interest. This will bring your CASH balance to -10.20.
