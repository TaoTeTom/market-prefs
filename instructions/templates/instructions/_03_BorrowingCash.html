In this experiment, you will be allowed to borrow CASH to purchase STOCK.    This happens when you enter a BUY order that costs more than the amount of CASH you have.    If you borrow, your CASH debt will appear as a negative amount.

You have 5000 in CASH and enter the order (BUY 6 shares at 1000 points).    This order will potentially cost you 6000 points.    Leaving you with a CASH balance of -1000 points.

If your CASH balance becomes negative, you will be charged 5% interest.    To continue the above example, at the end of the market period, you will be charged 1000 * 5% = 20 points in interest.    This will bring your CASH balance to -1020 points.
