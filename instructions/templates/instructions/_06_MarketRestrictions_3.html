In this experiment, the system will limit short sales to {{ float_ratio_cap }} of STOCK that is available to trade (this is called the "float"). This limit applies to the whole market, so if you attempt to short the STOCK and others have already shorted {{ float_ratio_cap }} of the float, your order will be canceled. The Market Page will inform you of the proportion of shorted STOCK.

Suppose there are 10 shares of STOCK available to trade, and two other participants have each shorted 5 shares. This means that 100% of the float has been shorted. In this situation, all SELL orders that short STOCK will be canceled.
