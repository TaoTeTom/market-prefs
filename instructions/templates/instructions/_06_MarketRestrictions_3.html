{{ block title }} MARKET RESTRICTIONS - Limit on Shorting {{ endblock }} {{ block content }}
In this experiment, the system will limit short sales to {{ float_ratio_cap }} of the STOCK that is available to trade (this is called the "float").    This limit applies to the whole market, so if you attempt to short the STOCK and others have already shorted {{ float_ratio_cap }} of the float, your order will be canceled.    The Market Page will inform you of the proportion of shorted STOCK.

Suppose there are 10 shares of STOCK available to trade, and two other traders have already shorted the STOCK by 5 shares each.    This means that 100% of the STOCK float has been shorted. If you attempt to sell one share short, that order will be canceled.
