A similar thing happens if you borrow CASH. This time the amount you borrowed must be less than 50% of the value of your STOCK at the current market price.

If you do not maintain that buffer, you will have one period to sell off enough STOCK to be within the 50% margin. When the market closes that period, if you still have too much debt, the system will attempt to sell your STOCK to decrease your CASH debt.

These automatic sell-offs can possibly happen at very low prices. So be careful when borrowing CASH.
