If your MARGIN drops below {{ marg_ratio_pct }} you will need to correct the situation.   If you borrowed CASH you will need to sell off STOCK.    If you shorted STOCK you will need to buy back shares.    These actions will increase your MARGIN.   You will be shown a warning message and advised on the proper course of action.

If you see such a warning you will be given one market period to increase your MARGIN.    If at the end of the market period, your margin is still below {{ marg_ratio_pct }}, the system will BUY or SELL shares on your behalf to correct your MARGIN.

Depending on market conditions, an automatic buy-in can happen at a very high price.   Also, a sell-off can happen at a very low price.    It is therefore advisable to maintain a proper MARGIN ratio to avoid this situation.
