A market period will consist of three parts

  • Market Page: This is the page where you will enter BUY and SELL orders. This page will be shown for 45 seconds and will automatically advance to the next page.
  • Forecasting Page: Here you will be asked to estimate what you believe the market price will be in the current round, the next round, and the round after that.
  • Period Results: This page will show the results of the period, including the new market price, whether you bought or sold STOCK, and interest and dividend earnings
