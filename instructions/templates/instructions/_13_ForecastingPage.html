After the Market page, is the Forecasting page.    Here you will be asked to predict the market price for the current period and the next two periods.    If your prediction for the current period is within {{ forecast_thold }} points of the actual value, you will be awarded an additional {{ forecast_reward }} points.    Note, only the current round forecast is considered for this award.

The example to the right shows what this page would look like in period 2.

After making your forecasts, use the Next button to progress to the period summary page.    The page will automatically advance to the summary page after 15 seconds.
